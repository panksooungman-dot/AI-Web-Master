# CRM Domain

## Purpose

This skill defines best practices for designing and implementing Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems.

The objective is to centralize customer information, manage sales opportunities, automate workflows, and improve customer engagement throughout the customer lifecycle.

---

# When to Use

Execute when:

- Building a CRM platform
- Managing customer relationships
- Tracking leads and opportunities
- Automating sales workflows
- Supporting customer success teams

---

# Objectives

Build CRM systems that are:

- Customer-centric
- Scalable
- Secure
- Data-driven
- Collaborative

# Contact Management

Manage:

- Contacts
- Organizations
- Roles
- Contact Information
- Communication History
- Relationship Mapping

Maintain a complete customer profile.

---

# Lead Management

Support:

- Lead Capture
- Qualification
- Assignment
- Scoring
- Conversion
- Nurturing

Track every lead from acquisition to conversion.

---

# Sales Pipeline

Define stages such as:

- New Lead
- Qualified
- Proposal
- Negotiation
- Won
- Lost

Every opportunity should have a measurable stage.

---

# Customer Activities

Track:

- Calls
- Emails
- Meetings
- Tasks
- Notes
- Follow-ups

Maintain a complete activity timeline.

---

# Workflow Automation

Automate:

- Lead Assignment
- Follow-up Reminders
- Email Notifications
- Task Creation
- Approval Workflows
- Status Updates

Reduce repetitive manual work.

---

# Reporting & Analytics

Measure:

- Lead Conversion Rate
- Sales Velocity
- Win Rate
- Pipeline Value
- Revenue Forecast
- Customer Retention
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

Use dashboards to support business decisions.

---

# Security

Implement:

- Role-Based Access Control
- Audit Logging
- Data Encryption
- Secure Authentication
- Data Privacy Controls

Protect customer information at all times.
