# ERP Domain

## Purpose

This skill defines best practices for designing and implementing Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems.

The objective is to integrate core business functions into a unified platform that improves operational efficiency, data consistency, and organizational visibility.

ERP systems should provide a single source of truth across business operations.

---

# When to Use

Execute when:

- Building an ERP platform
- Integrating enterprise business processes
- Managing finance, procurement, inventory, or HR
- Replacing disconnected business systems

---

# Objectives

Build ERP systems that are:

- Integrated
- Reliable
- Scalable
- Secure
- Auditable

Before completion verify:

- Core modules integrated
- Master data validated

# Failure Conditions

Stop and request clarification if:

- Business processes are undefined
- Financial requirements are incomplete
- Master data model is inconsistent
- Compliance requirements are unknown
- Integration scope is unclear

---

# Rules

- Maintain a single source of truth.
- Standardize business processes.
- Protect financial and employee data.
- Automate repetitive enterprise workflows.
- Preserve complete audit trails.
