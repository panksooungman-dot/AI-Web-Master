# Marketplace Domain

## Purpose

This skill defines best practices for designing and implementing multi-vendor marketplace platforms.

The objective is to enable trusted interactions between buyers and sellers while supporting listings, transactions, payments, fulfillment, dispute resolution, and platform governance.

---

# When to Use

Execute when:

- Building a marketplace platform
- Supporting multiple vendors
- Connecting buyers and sellers
- Managing commissions and payouts
- Implementing platform governance

---

# Objectives

Build marketplace platforms that are:

- Scalable
- Secure
- Trustworthy
- Transaction-focused
- Vendor-friendly

---

# Inputs

Expected inputs:

- Business Model
- Marketplace Type
- Vendor Requirements
- Buyer Journey
- Payment Requirements
- Compliance Requirements

# Vendor Management

Manage:

- Vendor Registration
- Verification
- Store Profiles
- Product Catalogs
- Performance Metrics
- Compliance Status

Every seller should have a verified identity.

---

# Buyer Experience

Provide:

- Product Discovery
- Search
- Filtering
- Recommendations
- Wishlists
- Secure Checkout

Optimize trust and conversion.

---

# Product Listings

Support:

- Categories
- Variants
- Images
- Pricing
- Inventory
- Availability

Moderate listings when necessary.

---

# Order Lifecycle

Track:

- Order Placement
- Payment
- Fulfillment
- Shipment
- Delivery
- Returns
- Refunds

Maintain complete order visibility.

---

# Payments & Payouts

Support:

- Buyer Payments
- Escrow (if applicable)
- Vendor Payouts
- Platform Commissions
- Refunds
- Tax Handling

Separate marketplace revenue from vendor revenue.

---

# Trust & Safety

Implement:

- Identity Verification
- Fraud Detection
- Ratings & Reviews
- Content Moderation
- Seller Reputation
- Buyer Protection

Promote a trustworthy ecosystem.

---

# Dispute Resolution

Provide workflows for:

- Refund Requests
- Order Disputes
- Policy Violations
- Seller Appeals
- Customer Support

Ensure fair and transparent resolution.

---

# Marketplace Analytics

Measure:

- GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)
- Active Buyers
- Active Sellers
- Conversion Rate
- Average Order Value
- Vendor Retention
- Commission Revenue

Monitor marketplace health continuously.

# Validation Checklist

Before completion verify:

- Vendor onboarding validated
- Listings moderated
- Payments tested

---

# Failure Conditions

Stop and request clarification if:

- Vendor policies are undefined
- Payment flows are incomplete
- Trust mechanisms are missing
- Marketplace rules are unclear
- Compliance requirements are incomplete

---

# Rules

- Protect buyers and sellers equally.
- Verify vendor identity.
- Maintain transparent transactions.
- Monitor marketplace quality continuously.
- Resolve disputes fairly.

---

# Success Criteria

This skill succeeds when:

- vendors can successfully sell products or services
- buyers complete purchases securely
- payouts are accurate
- trust mechanisms operate effectively
- marketplace growth is measurable
