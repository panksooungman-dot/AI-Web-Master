# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Purpose

This skill creates a Business Requirements Document (BRD) that captures business objectives, stakeholder expectations, and organizational needs before solution design begins.

The BRD serves as the business foundation for product planning and implementation.

---

# When to Use

Execute when:

- Initiating a new project
- Defining business objectives
- Gathering stakeholder requirements
- Documenting business processes
- Establishing project scope
- Preparing executive approval

---

# Objectives

Produce a BRD that is:

- Clear
- Business-focused
- Traceable
- Actionable
- Approved

---

# Inputs

Expected inputs:

- Business Goals
- Stakeholder Interviews
- Current Processes
- Regulatory Requirements
- Market Analysis
- Strategic Objectives

---

# BRD Structure

Generate the following sections:

- Executive Summary
- Business Background
- Business Objectives
- Problem Statement
- Current State Analysis
- Future State Vision
- Business Scope
- Out of Scope
- Stakeholders
- Business Requirements
- Business Rules
- Assumptions
- Constraints
- Risks
- Success Metrics
- Approval

---

# Business Requirements

Document:

- Functional Needs
- Operational Needs
- Reporting Requirements
- Compliance Requirements
- Process Improvements

Ensure every requirement supports a business objective.

---

# Business Rules

Define:

- Policies
- Constraints
- Decision Rules
- Approval Rules
- Validation Rules

Document rules independently from implementation.

---

# Stakeholders

Identify:

- Sponsors
- Product Owners
- Business Owners
- End Users
- Technical Teams
- External Partners

Define responsibilities for each stakeholder.

---

# Success Metrics

Measure:

- Business Value
- Cost Reduction
- Revenue Growth
- Process Efficiency
- Customer Satisfaction
- Operational Improvement

Use measurable KPIs.

---

# Validation Checklist

Verify:

- Objectives documented
- Scope defined
- Stakeholders identified
- Requirements validated
- Risks assessed
- Approval obtained

---

# Failure Conditions

Stop if:

- Business objectives are unclear
- Stakeholders disagree
- Scope is undefined
- Success metrics are unavailable
- Requirements conflict

---

# Outputs

Generate:

- Business Requirements Document
- Stakeholder Register
- Business Rules Catalog
- Executive Summary

---

# Rules

- Focus on business outcomes.
- Avoid technical implementation details.
- Keep requirements measurable.
- Validate with stakeholders.
- Maintain traceability.

---

# Success Criteria

This skill succeeds when:

- business goals are clearly defined
- stakeholders approve the document
- requirements support strategic objectives
- project scope is understood
